# Trademark And Brand Policy

UI Backlot contains editable demo surfaces that reconstruct public software
interfaces from local captures, donor references, and source-authentic assets.
The project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Anthropic,
OpenAI, Apple, Microsoft, Figma, Adobe, Airtable, or any other product owner
referenced in examples or docs.

Product names such as Claude, Codex, macOS, Finder, PowerPoint, Word, Excel,
Figma, Premiere, and Airtable are used descriptively so contributors and agents
can understand which real-world workflow a surface represents. Those names and
their marks remain the property of their respective owners.

## Contributor Rules

- Source-authentic fonts, icons, logos, and UI atoms may be tracked when they
  materially improve fidelity. Document the source/provenance in the asset
  folder and relevant registry entry.
- Prefer editable HTML/CSS/SVG reconstructions over screenshots for controls
  that need to be scripted, styled, or animated.
- Keep private captures, downloaded launch videos, extracted frames, copied
  full app bundles, and donor-repo clones out of git unless a small reviewed
  subset is intentionally selected for source-authentic fidelity.
- When a surface is visually informed by a third-party product, document the
  source evidence and asset decision in `surfaces/registry.json`.
- If a demo is for public distribution, review the final video and copy for
  brand confusion before shipping it.
