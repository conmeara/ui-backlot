# Trademark And Brand Policy

UI Backlot contains hand-authored, editable demo surfaces inspired by public
software interfaces and local reference captures. The project is not affiliated
with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Anthropic, OpenAI, Apple, Microsoft, Figma,
Adobe, Airtable, or any other product owner referenced in examples or docs.

Product names such as Claude, Codex, macOS, Finder, PowerPoint, Word, Excel,
Figma, Premiere, and Airtable are used descriptively so contributors and agents
can understand which real-world workflow a surface represents. Those names and
their marks remain the property of their respective owners.

## Contributor Rules

- Do not copy proprietary product code, app bundles, icon files, fonts, logos,
  screenshots, or video frames into tracked source unless the license clearly
  permits redistribution.
- Prefer hand-authored HTML/CSS/SVG approximations for reusable demo surfaces.
- Keep private captures, downloaded launch videos, extracted frames, copied
  fonts, and donor-repo clones out of git unless their redistribution rights are
  clear and documented.
- When a surface is visually informed by a third-party product, document the
  source evidence and asset decision in `surfaces/registry.json`.
- If a demo is for public distribution, review the final video and copy for
  brand confusion before shipping it.
